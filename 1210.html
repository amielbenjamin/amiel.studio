1210 Pop-Up

#scenography #exhibition design #event management #curation #photography

Lead on scenography and curation, I transformed a local space into an urban hub for the football and culture club 1210 Wien. The project turned a traditional photo exhibition into a three-day community event (Nov 6–8, 2025), integrating local musical talent with a distinct visual language that celebrates the intersection of sport, art, and district identity.

Working as the lead project manager and scenographer, I designed this exhibition to serve as a physical manifesto for the club’s motto: "Fussball. Kunst. Kultur." Hosted at Schleifgasse 11, the project aimed to create a low-threshold cultural space that feels as authentic as a locker room but as curated as a contemporary gallery.

Immersive Scenography. The room design translated football’s visual DNA into an urban gallery setting, featuring high-contrast black-and-white checkered walls, yellow accents, and a football net suspended across the ceiling to define the space.

Curated Event Series. Rather than a static display, the exhibition functioned as a stage. I curated a lineup of local music artists who performed live, transforming the viewing experience into a vibrant social event that attracted a diverse neighborhood audience.

Tactile Installation. To bridge the gap between art and play, I integrated a patch of artificial turf with a goal-like setup and footballs, encouraging visitors of all ages—from local children to club veterans—to physically interact with the space.

Visual Documentation. The walls showcased portraits and snapshots of the 1210 community, documenting the faces and places that define the club’s presence in Vienna’s 21st district.

Identity & Merch. The exhibition also functioned as a flagship for the club’s aesthetic, incorporating a presentation of "1210 Wien" merchandise (shirts, sweaters) and local products (branded wine), creating a holistic brand experience for the visitors.

Social Cohesion. By blending sport-related visuals with artistic performances, the project successfully created a meeting point for different social groups, fostering community pride and cultural exchange within Floridsdorf.
